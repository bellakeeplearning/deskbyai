Do You Need a Foot Rest Under Your Desk?

2026-06-26 4 min read

When You Need One. If your feet don't rest flat on the floor at the right chair height, you need a foot rest. Dangling feet round your lower back and compress thighs against the seat edge.

Benefits. Reduces lower back strain, hip pressure, and circulation issues. For shorter individuals, it's essential.

How to Choose. Look for angle adjustability, 4-6 inch height range, non-slip surface. Some have built-in rocking motion for micro-movement.
