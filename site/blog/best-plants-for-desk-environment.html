7 Low-Maintenance Desk Plants That Improve Air Quality

2026-07-07 6 min read

1. Snake Plant -- water every 2-3 weeks, tolerates low light, purifies air.

2. ZZ Plant -- thrives on neglect, glossy dark leaves.

3. Pothos -- trailing plant, grows quickly, easy to propagate.

4. Succulent -- small, drought-resistant, needs bright light.

5. Monstera -- statement plant with large split leaves.

6. Spider Plant -- easy to keep alive, purifies air.

7. Air Plant -- no soil needed, mist 2-3 times per week.

Place plants at the corner of your desk, 6-12 inches from your monitor, in natural light direction.
