Monitor Light Bar vs Desk Lamp: Which Is Better?

2026-07-04 5 min read

Monitor Light Bar wins for computer work. Attaches to screen, no glare, zero desk space.

Desk Lamp wins for reading and writing. Directional light, wider coverage, higher brightness.

Best solution: both. Light bar for screen work, desk lamp for reading. Total cost roughly $50-80.
