How to Reduce Screen Glare: 5 Practical Fixes

2026-07-05 5 min read

1. Desk Perpendicular to Window -- natural light from the side eliminates glare.

2. Tilt Monitor Slightly Down -- 10-20 degrees changes reflection angle.

3. Monitor Light Bar -- never creates screen glare.

4. Sheer Curtains -- control natural light without darkening room.

5. Anti-Glare Screen Filter -- matte filter diffuses reflections.
