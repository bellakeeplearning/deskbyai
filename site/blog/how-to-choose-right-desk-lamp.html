How to Choose the Right Desk Lamp: Complete Buyer Guide

2026-07-13 6 min read

1. Color Temperature. Look for adjustable 2700K-5000K. Warm (2700K) for evening, cool (5000K) for focus.

2. Brightness. Your desk should be 500-750 lux. The lamp should be dimmable so you can adjust.

3. Beam Angle. Wide beam (120 degrees) for general desk lighting. Narrow beam for task lighting.

4. Size. Monitor light bars take zero space. Arm lamps need 6-8 inches of desk edge. Traditional lamps need 8-12 inches of surface area.

5. Glare. Monitor light bars don't create screen glare. Traditional lamps should be positioned to the side, not behind or in front of the screen.
